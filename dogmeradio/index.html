Dogmefilm is dead! Long live dogmeradio!

The proposed rules of Dogmeradio:

  1. No editing!
  2. No music unless it is naturally occuring (playing on the radio in the background for instance)
  3. No interviews by appointment. Only talk to people you happen to meet.
  4. No voiceover.

Mmmmm, podcasting!
